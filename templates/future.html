Future Work

For this project we focused on Content Based Filtering mechanisms to create playlists, and in the future, Collaborative Filtering (CF) mechanisms can be added for a more holistic understanding of the user. CF uses the user's preferences and ratings and similarities between users' tastes, ultimately sometimes leading to unexpected songs being added to playlists.

Also, we want to be able to build a model that can classify songs and assign them to playlists purely based on audio features. Indeed, playlist generation has a lot of uncontrollable factors outside of our control (i.e. user's mood, taste, etc), but to provide a generic solution to playlist generation purely via song characteristics will be the next step.

Lastly, another interesting step might be generating "Top 50" playlists for a country, purely from scratch. By finding songs most frequently appearing in playlists and widely-listened to, coupled with using the song features, it would be an interesting extension to explore to recreate "Top 50" playlists for different countries based on each countries popularity.
